Frequently Asked Questions

Preguntas Frecuentes

Q: Is CitaCoin a cryptocurrency?

No. CitaCoin is a loyalty rewards currency that only exists within the CitaPros platform. It's more like airline miles than Bitcoin.

Q: Do my coins expire?

No — as long as your CitaPros account is active, your coins never expire.

Q: When do I receive my referral commission?

Referral coins are held for 30 days to confirm the subscriber remains active, then released automatically. Cash tier payouts are issued monthly.

Q: What happens to my coins if I cancel?

Your coins are tied to your active account. Canceling forfeits your balance, your locked-in rate, and your referral tier status.

Q: Can I refer someone who already uses CitaPros?

No — the referral program only applies to new subscribers who haven't used CitaPros before.

No. CitaCoin es una moneda de recompensas de lealtad que solo existe dentro de la plataforma CitaPros. Es más como millas de aerolínea que Bitcoin.

Las monedas de referencia se retienen durante 30 días para confirmar que el suscriptor permanece activo, luego se liberan automáticamente.

No — el programa de referencias solo aplica a nuevos suscriptores que no hayan usado CitaPros antes.
